Saving Lives by Learning Knots.

As with Climbing knots, the emphasis for SAR is on reliability and safety. It is also desirable that each knot can be untied reasonably easily. Learn how to tie these useful survival knots for outdoors and bushcraft. These are knots every prepper should learn to tie and may be useful in bug out situations so it would be good to commit many of these knots to memory. On the plus side, it is extremely important that search and rescue teams are on top of their knowledge when performing their duties!
